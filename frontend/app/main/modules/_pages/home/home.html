Thank you for visiting Lolcomp.net! This is our alpha release. To use Lolcomp, first add champions to each team by using the search field above each list.

Synergy will tell you all* mechanical interactions between the selected and its team members.

Counters will tell you all* mechanical interactions between the selected and the entire enemy team

* Mechanical interactions are defined as relations on this website. These relations are created based off a set of rules than can be seen Here. (currently only 2 rules about channel skills, knockup, and synergy with Yasuo R)
